Volunteers and Internship Program

Volunteers and internship program gives both local and international volunteers / interns opportunity to volunteer various projects. The program search work placement for volunteers within the organization and our partners in Kenya.

Our volunteer programs are highly regarded for safe projects based on attention to detail, affordability, professional field volunteer support and diverse opportunities for cultural immersion. We are committed to our quality and safe volunteer community programs.

Regularly, we review volunteer projects, host families, field support staff and other logistics to ensure our programs are the best they can be. We recruit volunteers to various projects according to their area interest/skills and taste.

We currently have 25 volunteers,23 from Kenya,2 from columia.
